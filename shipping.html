Re-imagining

Consumer Shipping

Opening a self-serve digital channel for a carrier built on business accounts — removing the barriers that kept one-time consumers from shipping online.

Problem

▼

A shipping carrier offered consumer shipping, but their digital tools required a business account number. Consumers shipping occasionally had no path online — driving them to competitors or the counter.

A second challenge: the pricing engine required accurate package dimensions, and consumers are often unsure of how to properly measure package dimensions — leading to errors and friction once the package was inducted into the shipping system.

Constraints

▼
  • Backend pricing engine required exact dimensions and weight — no workaround
  • Consumers unfamiliar with measuring packages accurately
  • No account creation — had to work as a fully guest experience
  • Single-page experience required — no multi-step wizard or redirect
  • Payment infrastructure needed to support one-time unregistered transactions

My Contribution

▼

Flows

Mapped the full consumer journey from package type selection through to payment, identifying where business-account assumptions created drop-off in the existing experience.

Interaction Logic

Designed progressive disclosure to reduce cognitive load — revealing the next step only once the previous was complete, keeping a single long page from feeling overwhelming.

Service Design

Worked with operations to define two standard package presets — envelope and box — with pre-set dimensions, so consumers only needed to input weight, the one value they could reliably provide.

Platform Change

Defined requirements for a guest shipping flow — a net-new capability requiring new identity handling and payment infrastructure for one-time transactions without registration.

How It Worked

▼

The tool kept the entire experience on one page using progressive disclosure. Consumers entered origin and destination addresses, selected a package type — envelope or box — and were shown a visual guide for measuring. Standard envelope dimensions were pre-filled; box users entered their own L×W×H. Once weight was added, the pricing engine returned a real-time quote, and payment was completed without an account.

Outcome

▼

Successfully launched a net-new digital consumer shipping channel — opening a new line of revenue for the company. The single-page design with visual measurement guidance reduced drop-off from consumers unsure how to size their packages, and removing the account requirement eliminated the primary barrier to conversion for occasional shippers.
