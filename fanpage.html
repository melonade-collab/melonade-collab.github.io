📍 The Basics

Full Name:Dru Hill
Origin:Baltimore, Maryland 🇺🇸
Genre:R&B, Soul
Record Label:Island Records / Def Jam
Active Since:1992
Members:Sisqó · Nokio · Jazz · Woody
Named After:Druid Hill Park, Baltimore

📖 Their Story

Dru Hill formed in Baltimore, Maryland in 1992 when four friends — Sisqó, Nokio, Jazz, and Woody — came together with a shared love of gospel, soul, and R&B harmony. They named themselves after Druid Hill Park in their hometown, and that local pride has always been part of who they are.

They broke onto the national scene in 1996 with their self-titled debut album, and "Tell Me" put them on the map instantly. Their silky four-part harmonies and heartfelt lyrics set them apart from everyone else in the R&B game at the time. I remember hearing them for the first time and literally stopping everything I was doing!! 😭

In 1998 they dropped Enter the Dru — which in my humble opinion is a PERFECT album from start to finish!! "How Deep Is Your Love," "These Are the Times," "We're Not Making Love No More"... every single song is a masterpiece!!

🎤 The Members 🎤

Four incredible voices · One legendary group

♪ · ♫ · ♪ · ♫ · ♪ · ♫ · ♪ · ♫ · ♪
👑
Sisqó
Mark Andrews · Lead vocals
The flashy frontman with the silver hair!! Sisqó's voice is unreal — that range!! He writes most of their songs and brings SO much energy to everything. He has solo projects too and honestly everything he touches is gold!! 💛
🎹
Nokio
Tamir Ruffin · Producer / vocals
The creative genius behind the scenes!! Nokio produces a lot of their tracks and his ear for melody is incredible. He's also got a beautiful voice — every Dru Hill harmony hits different because of him!! 🙌
🎶
Jazz
Larry Anthony Jr. · Tenor
Jazz brings the warmth to their sound!! His tenor voice is the thread that holds all four harmonies together. When he sings those high notes in "These Are the Times" I literally cannot cope!! 😭😭
🎸
Woody
James Green · Bass vocals
The foundation of the whole group!! Woody's bass voice gives Dru Hill that deep, rich sound that makes their harmonies so distinctive. You literally FEEL it in your chest!! He is so underrated tbh!! 🔥

Together these four voices create something that NO ONE else can replicate!! Baltimore raised them right!! 🙏
